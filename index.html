About Us

Say-Sili Chips are among the best chip snacks in Pamantasan ng Cabuyao (University of Cabuyao), It is a vegetable-based snack with a unique flavor. Say-Sili Chips comes in a variety of flavors, including Cheese, Barbeque, and Original. Say-Sili Chps, a product of Chipsie Corporation, are distinctive, delicious, affordable, and health-promoting. We also want it to taste exactly like regular chips, but with a different kind of vegetable. Say-Sili was created for students who don't like to eat vegetables given that it looks and tastes a lot like regular chips. If you'd rather eat healthy snacks, this is an alternative. Say-Sili is ready to provide you with high-quality flavors and taste, and is available in Pamantasan ng Cabuyao. The team members of Chipsie Corporation are a group of people with various experiences in food processing. This product is healthy, safe, and beneficial to all users. Let's eat veggies to stay healthy.
